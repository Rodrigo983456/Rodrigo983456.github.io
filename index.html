Peterborough Cathedral is in the heart of the historic city of Peterborough, England. There has been a place of worship and prayer on the site for 1350 years, the present building approaching its 900th anniversary. With superb Gothic West Front and beautiful hand-painted wooden nave ceiling.

Meadows Country Park

England
Explore More

Meadows Country Park is a charming natural retreat nestled in the beautiful city of Peterborough, located in the heart of Cambridgeshire, England. It is a haven of tranquility, where visitors can escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse themselves in the beauty of nature. The park spans across a sprawling area, offering an impressive array of landscapes to explore. It boasts vast meadows, serene woodlands, and picturesque lakes, all teeming with a rich variety of flora and fauna. The park is meticulously maintained, ensuring that visitors can enjoy a pristine environment while engaging in various recreational activities.

Railworld Wildlife Haven

England
Explore More

Railworld Wildlife Haven is a unique and captivating conservation project located in Peterborough, Cambridgeshire, England. It is a haven for both wildlife enthusiasts and those passionate about environmental conservation. This hidden gem combines the charm of a railway heritage center with the beauty of a wildlife sanctuary, creating a truly one-of-a-kind experience for visitors. The site encompasses a diverse range of habitats, including lush wetlands, meadows, and woodlands, which have been carefully developed to provide a sanctuary for various species of animals and plants. Railworld Wildlife Haven places a strong emphasis on promoting biodiversity and ecological sustainability, making it an important sanctuary for local wildlife.
